# Advans IT Solutions GmbH

> **We deliver technology to your business.**

We are a Swiss IT solutions provider based in **Basel**. We advise our clients
on technical challenges and deliver services that range from consulting all the
way to taking ownership of complete projects — from analysis and design through
to implementation and operations.

## What we do

- **IT Solutions & Projects** — We design and realize individual, future-proof
  IT infrastructures tailored to your business processes.
- **Life Science IT** — Specialized IT solutions for the life science industry.
- **Advans IT Education** — Internal and external training to bring knowledge
  and new technologies into your organization.
- **Shop** — Notebooks, gadgets, and more in our online shop.

## How we work

Our experts combine technical expertise with industry know-how to adapt IT
infrastructures to your processes and to continuously evolving technology
standards. Our clients are at the center of everything we do — we deliver
client-oriented, individual solutions.
